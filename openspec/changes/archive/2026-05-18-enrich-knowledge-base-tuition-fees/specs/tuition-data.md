# Spec: Tuition Fee Knowledge Entries

## Coverage
Go8 universities, international research students (PhD + Masters by Research), 2025-2026 data.

## Entry Template
Each entry ~300-500 words covering:
- University name + year
- PhD annual fee range by faculty/discipline band
- Masters by Research annual fee range
- RTP (Research Training Program) / scholarship fee offset info
- Fee estimator URL
- Official source URL
- Data year disclaimer
